About Me

Hello! My name is Beinuo Zhou, a senior undergraduate student majoring in School of Ocean Engineering and Technology at Sun Yat-sen University. My research focuses on ocean dynamics, air-sea interaction, and estuarine and coastal processes. I am passionate about advancing our understanding of marine science through rigorous research and exploration.
